About Raptors Club

Established on November 2, 2022, the Raptors Club at MIT University provides students with hands-on experience in aeronautical design, including Fixed Wing, Rotary Wing, and Rocketry. Our goal is to bridge the gap between theoretical learning and practical applications in the field of aeronautics.

We encourage innovation, teamwork, and research in aerospace technology, offering students opportunities to participate in national and international competitions.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: To provide students with hands-on aeronautical experience and foster innovation in aviation and aerospace technology.

Vision: To be a leading university club in aeronautics, producing top engineers and innovators in the aerospace industry.

Our Achievements

  • Conducted Glide Quest 25, a successful aerodynamics event.
  • Organized a hands-on Mission Planner workshop.
  • Hosted an extensive Rocket Building Workshop.
  • Conducted CAD in Aero Modelling training sessions.
