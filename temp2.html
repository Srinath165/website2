About Raptors Club

Raptors Club, established on November 2, 2022, provides students with hands-on experience in Fixed Wing, Rotary Wing, and Rocketry. The club serves as a platform for aspiring aerospace enthusiasts to apply classroom knowledge to real-world projects, bridging the gap between theory and practical implementation.

Members engage in designing, building, and testing aerostructures, gaining expertise in aerodynamics, propulsion systems, structural analysis, and flight mechanics. Through collaborative projects, workshops, and competitions, students develop problem-solving skills, innovation, and teamwork, preparing them for careers in aerospace engineering and related fields.
