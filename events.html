Our Events

We conduct various hands-on workshops and competitions to enhance students' practical aeronautical skills.

Glide Quest 25

A thrilling aerodynamics competition where participants designed and tested gliders for maximum flight efficiency. Teams were judged on stability, range, and innovation.

Mission Planner Workshop

A deep dive into UAV mission planning using advanced simulation software. Attendees learned flight path optimization and real-time telemetry analysis.

Rocket Building Workshop

Participants explored the fundamentals of rocket propulsion, designing and launching their own model rockets with real aeronautical principles.

CAD in Aero Modelling

A hands-on session on designing aerospace components using Computer-Aided Design (CAD) software. Ideal for students interested in aircraft design.
